Kevin Durant

Professional NBA Player for Brooklyn Nets

Plays Forward postion and wears the #7 jersey Kevin lives by these words: Hard work beats talent when talent fails to work hard It's hard to play well, but it's easy to work hard Kevin Durant Is one of the NBA's greatest talents that the NBA has ever seen. He stands at 7-Feet tall with a wingspan of 7 feet, 5 inches. He is one of the most dominant players in the NBA averaging 27 points a game, 7 rebounds, and 4 assists a game. During his rookie season he also was awarded the Rookie of the year award. His athletic abilities are greater than most NBA players, you can see that he is the very dominant player and will be considered the greatest of all time.
